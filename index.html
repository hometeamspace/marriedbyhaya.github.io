About Me

Hey there, I’m Haya!

A young, fun, authentic marriage celebrant based in Canberra. In my not-so-spare time, I am a lawyer and owner of half-finished craft projects.

My favourite part of every wedding is the ceremony. Witnessing couples as they share their stories of how they met, laugh about their inside jokes, and talk about their future hopes and dreams is so special and sacred. As a celebrant, being able to share these moments with couples on their wedding day is an absolute honour.

If you can’t already tell, I will absolutely love getting to know every detail of your love journey – over coffee, wine, video-call, you name it! I strongly believe that your ceremony should be uniquely YOU, and so I’ll use this information to create a personalised wedding ceremony, interwoven with any readings, poems or rituals suited to you and your vision.
